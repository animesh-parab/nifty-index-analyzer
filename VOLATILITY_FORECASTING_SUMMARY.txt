================================================================================
📊 FORECASTING METHODS
================================================================================

1. HISTORICAL VOLATILITY (25% weight)
   - Standard log returns volatility
   - Simple and reliable baseline

2. PARKINSON VOLATILITY (20% weight)
   - High-Low range based
   - Captures intraday volatility

3. GARMAN-KLASS VOLATILITY (25% weight)
   - OHLC based estimator
   - Most statistically efficient

4. ATR-BASED VOLATILITY (15% weight)
   - Average True Range normalized
   - Popular among traders

5. GARCH(1,1) FORECAST (15% weight)
   - Forward-looking prediction
   - Adapts to regime changes

6. ENSEMBLE FORECAST (100%)
   - Weighted average of all methods
   - Most robust and reliable

================================================================================
🎨 VOLATILITY REGIMES
================================================================================

VERY LOW (0-0.5%)    → Sell options, collect premium
LOW (0.5-1.0%)       → Sell strangles/straddles
NORMAL (1.0-1.5%)    → Balanced approach
ELEVATED (1.5-2.5%)  → Reduce positions
HIGH (2.5-4.0%)      → Buy options, avoid selling
EXTREME (>4.0%)      → Protective strategies only
