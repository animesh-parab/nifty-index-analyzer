  TROUBLESHOOTING

PROBLEM: Too many stop loss hits (>50%)
SOLUTION:
  • Increase STOP_LOSS_PERCENT to 0.75% or 1%
  • Improve prediction accuracy (retrain XGBoost)
  • Increase MIN_CONFIDENCE_TO_TRADE to 65%

PROBLEM: Too few take profit hits (<50%)
SOLUTION:
  • Decrease TAKE_PROFIT_PERCENT to 0.6%
  • Check if VIX is low (market not moving)
  • Consider shorter timeframes (15min vs 30min)

PROBLEM: Low profit factor despite good win rate
SOLUTION:
  • Verify stop losses are being honored
  • Check exit reason breakdown
  • Ensure RISK_REWARD_RATIO is 1.5

PROBLEM: Hourly analysis shows no data
SOLUTION:
  • Run dashboard during market hours first
  • Wait for predictions to be verified (15 min)
  • Need at least 50+ predictions with outcomes
