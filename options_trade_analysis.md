## 🎯 What Went Wrong

### Critical Mistakes:

1. **Entry After High**
   - High of day was at 9:15 AM
   - Entry at 9:29 AM = chasing after the move
   - Should have entered BEFORE the high

2. **Strike Too Far OTM**
   - 105 points OTM at entry
   - Needed 4.3% move in Nifty just to reach ATM
   - Too ambitious for intraday

3. **No Momentum**
   - Spot moved only 59 points in 63 minutes
   - Not enough for OTM call to gain value
   - Theta decay working against the position

4. **Exit Signal Correct**
   - Exit at 10:32 AM was the right call
   - Trade setup invalidated
   - Prevented further losses

---

## ✅ Lessons Learned

### For Future Trades:

1. **Don't Chase After High**
   - If high is already made, avoid OTM calls
   - Wait for pullback or skip the trade

2. **Strike Selection**
   - For intraday: Max 50-100 points OTM
   - 105 points OTM needs strong momentum
   - Consider ATM or slightly ITM for safer trades

3. **Entry Timing**
   - Enter BEFORE the big move, not after
   - 9:29 AM entry was too late (high at 9:15 AM)

4. **Respect Exit Signals**
   - Exit at 10:32 AM prevented bigger loss
   - Good risk management

5. **Premium Trigger**
   - "Buy above ₹195" was smart
   - Trade never triggered = no loss
   - Conditional entry saved capital
